Introduction


What is this website for?


The purpose of this website is to give you - water industry professionals, researchers and students, a quick and efficient tool to determine the baseflow of a karst system.


How do you use it?


  1. Upload your input data (.csv file)
  2. Click on "process data"
  3. You are done!

What are the output?


  1. Multiple charts
  2. Downloadable output file
